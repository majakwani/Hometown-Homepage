Visit Gulshan

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Karachi.

Top three activities to do at Gulshan

Attend a show or a concert

The Gulshan Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in the local park

The Gulshan part is perfect for picnic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Gulshan field.

Play volleyball, football, basketball or lift some weights.
